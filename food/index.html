산들애건강밥상: Ttoekbokki

Delicious healthy food. You need to order when you get a ticket to wait in line so be organized. Wasn't a long wait. Lots of side dishes and a really succulent spicy octopus. The potato pancake is their thing and is delicious and easy to eat a lot of!
